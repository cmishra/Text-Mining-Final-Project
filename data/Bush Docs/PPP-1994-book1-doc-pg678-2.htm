Nomination for U.S. Attorney for New Jersey
April 12, 1994

    The President today nominated Faith S. Hochberg as the U.S. Attorney 
for the District of New Jersey.
    ``Faith Hochberg's legal skills and dedication to law enforcement 
make her an excellent candidate for this position and will serve the 
State of New Jersey well,'' the President said.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
