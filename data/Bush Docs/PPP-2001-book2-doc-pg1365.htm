Message to the Congress Transmitting Reports of the Department of 
Transportation
November 8, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the Department of Transportation's Calendar Year 
1999 reports on Activities Under the National Traffic and Motor Vehicle 
Safety Act of 1966, the Highway Safety Act of 1966, and the Motor 
Vehicle Information and Cost Savings Act of 1972.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 November 8, 2001.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
November 9.
