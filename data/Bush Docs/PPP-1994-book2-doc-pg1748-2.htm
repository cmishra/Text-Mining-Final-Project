Statement on the National Award for Museum Service
October 13, 1994

    This national honor is a tribute to the power of museums to engage 
children, families, and communities, in towns and cities all across 
America.

Note: This statement was included in a White House statement announcing 
the presentation of the National Award for Museum Service to the Brukner 
Nature Center, Troy, OH; the Cummer Museum of Art and Gardens, 
Jacksonville, FL; and the Missouri Historical Society, St. Louis, MO.
