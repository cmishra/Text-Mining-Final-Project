Nomination for Ambassador to Tunisia
May 11, 1994

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Mary Ann 
Casey, of Colorado, as Ambassador to the Republic of Tunisia.
    ``Mary Ann Casey's extensive foreign service experience will be a 
great asset in her role as Ambassador to Tunisia,'' the President said. 
``I am delighted to announce her nomination.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
