Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Telecommunications 
Payments to Cuba
March 6, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 1705(e)(6) of the Cuban Democracy Act of 
1992, as amended by section 102(g) of the Cuban Liberty and Democratic 
Solidarity (LIBERTAD) Act of 1996, Public Law 104-114, 110 Stat. 785, 22 
U.S.C. 6004(e)(6), I transmit herewith a semiannual report detailing 
payments made to Cuba by United States persons as a result of the 
provision of telecommunications services pursuant to

[[Page 201]]

Department of the Treasury specific licenses.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 March 6, 2001.
