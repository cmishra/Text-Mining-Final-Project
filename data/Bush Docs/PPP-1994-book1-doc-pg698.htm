Nomination for an Under Secretary of the Treasury
April 14, 1994

    The President today announced the nomination of Assistant Treasury 
Secretary Ronald K. Noble as Treasury Under Secretary for Enforcement.
    ``I am pleased to nominate Ron to this newly created and critically 
important position,'' the President said. ``I am confident that his 
proven leadership and skilled service in the area of law enforcement 
will continue to contribute greatly to our fight against crime.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
