Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Proliferation of
Weapons of Mass Destruction
June 14, 2000

To the Congress of the United States:
    Enclosed is a report to the Congress on Executive Order 12938, as 
required by section 204 of the International Emergency Economic Powers 
Act (50 U.S.C. 1703(c)) and section 401(c) of the National Emergencies 
Act (50 U.S.C. 1641(c)).

                                                      William J. Clinton

 The White House,

 June 14, 2000.
