Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Prevention of 
Nuclear Proliferation
April 19, 2000

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    As required under section 601(a) of the Nuclear Non-Proliferation 
Act of 1978, as amended (Public Law 95-242, 22 U.S.C. 3281(a)), I am 
transmitting a report on the United States Government's efforts to 
prevent nuclear proliferation. The report covers activities between 
January 1, 1998, and December 31, 1998.
         Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

 Note:  Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
