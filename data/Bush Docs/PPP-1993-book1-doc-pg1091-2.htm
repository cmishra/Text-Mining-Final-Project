Nomination for Inspector General of the United States Information Agency
July 16, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate attorney 
Marian Bennett as Inspector General at the United States Information 
Agency.
    ``I am pleased today to name Marian Bennett to this important post 
at USIA,'' the President said. ``I am certain she will work hard to 
ensure USIA is a tight-run operation up to the task of its mission to 
promote democracy and freedom abroad.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
