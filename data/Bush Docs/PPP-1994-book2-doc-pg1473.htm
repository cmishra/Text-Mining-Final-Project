Nomination for United States District Court Judges
August 16, 1994

    The President today nominated four individuals to serve on the U.S. 
District Court: Elaine F. Bucklo, David H. Coar, and Robert W. Gettleman 
for the Northern District of Illinois, and Paul E. Riley for the 
Southern District of Illinois.
    ``These nominees will bring legal talent and dedication to the 
Federal bench,'' the President said. ``I know they will serve our 
country with distinction.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
