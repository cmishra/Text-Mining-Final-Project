Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Final Report on the 
National Emergency With Respect to Angola
August 4, 2003

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    Consistent with section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act, 50 
U.S.C. 1641(c), and section 204(c) of the International Emergency 
Economic Powers Act, 50 U.S.C. 1703(c), I am transmitting a final report 
prepared by my Administration on the national emergency with respect to 
the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA) that was 
declared in Executive Order 12865 of September 26, 1993, and terminated 
in Executive Order 13298 of May 6, 2003.
         Sincerely,

                                                          George W. Bush

Note: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Richard B. Cheney, President of the 
Senate. This letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
August 5.
