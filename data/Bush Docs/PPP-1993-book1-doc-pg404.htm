Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Hazardous Materials 
Transportation
April 5, 1993

To the Congress of the United States:
    In accordance with the requirements of section 109(e) of the 
Hazardous Materials Transportation Act (Public Law 93-633); 49 U.S.C. 
108(e)), I transmit herewith the Annual Report on Hazardous Materials 
Transportation for calendar year 1991.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,
April 5, 1993.
