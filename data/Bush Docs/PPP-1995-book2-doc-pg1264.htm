Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Haiti
August 24, 1995

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    As required by section 107 of Public Law 104-6, I transmit herewith 
the report on the cumulative incremental cost of all United States 
activities in Haiti subsequent to September 30, 1993.
        Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Newt Gingrich, Speaker of the House 
of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
