Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the National Institute 
of Building Sciences
July 27, 2000

To the Congress of the United States:
    In accordance with the requirements of section 809 of the Housing 
and Community Development Act of 1974, as amended (12 U.S.C. 1701j-
2(j)), I transmit herewith the annual report of the National Institute 
of Building Sciences for fiscal year 1998.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

July 27, 2000.
