Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Implementation 
of NAFTA
December 27, 1993

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    Pursuant to Section 101(b)(1)(B) of the North American Free Trade 
Agreement Implementation Act, I am pleased to submit the attached report 
regarding the implementation of the North American Free Trade Agreement.
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate. 
This letter was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
December 28.
