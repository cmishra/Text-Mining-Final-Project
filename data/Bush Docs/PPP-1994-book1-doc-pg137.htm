Appointment for a Deputy Assistant Secretary of Veterans Affairs
January 26, 1994

    The President today appointed Gil Coronado, a retired Air Force 
Colonel with service in Vietnam, to be Deputy Assistant Secretary of 
Veterans Affairs for Legislative Affairs.
    ``Gil Coronado has served this country with distinction for more 
than 30 years,'' said the President. ``I think he will do an outstanding 
job helping to steer our veterans' legislation through Congress.''

Note: A biography of the appointee was made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
