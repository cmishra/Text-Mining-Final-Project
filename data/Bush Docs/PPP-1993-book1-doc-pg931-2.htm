Nomination for Ambassador to Mongolia
June 24, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Donald 
Johnson, a career member of the Foreign Service, to be Ambassador to 
Mongolia.
    ``Donald Johnson has served our country with distinction for almost 
two decades in the Foreign Service,'' said the President. ``I am pleased 
that he will be taking this ambassadorial post.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
