Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Railroad Safety
March 8, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the 1993 annual report on the Administration of 
the Federal Railroad Safety Act of 1970, pursuant to section 211 of the 
Act (45 U.S.C. 440(a)).

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

March 8, 1995.
