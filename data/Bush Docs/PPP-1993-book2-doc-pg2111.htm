Appointment for Environmental Protection Agency Regional Administrators
December 3, 1993

    The President today approved John H. Hankinson, Jr., as Regional 
Administrator, Region IV, and Jane N. Saginaw as Regional Administrator, 
Region VI, at the Environmental Protection Agency.
    ``I am pleased today to name these two hard-working individuals to 
our team at EPA,'' the President said.

Note: Biographies of the appointees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary. This item was not received in time for publication 
in the appropriate issue.
