Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the National 
Endowment for Democracy
March 18, 1999

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by the provisions of section 504(h) of Public Law 98-
164, as amended (22 U.S.C. 4413(i)), I transmit herewith the 15th Annual 
Report of the National Endowment for Democracy, which covers fiscal year 
1998.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

March 18, 1999.
