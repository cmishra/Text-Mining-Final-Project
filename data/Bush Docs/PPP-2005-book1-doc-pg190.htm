Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Implementation of the 
Plan for Securing Nuclear Weapons, Material, and Expertise of the States 
of the Former Soviet Union
February 8, 2005

To the Congress of the United States:
    Consistent with section 1205 of the National Defense Authorization 
Act for Fiscal Year 2003 (Public Law 107-314), I am providing a report 
prepared by my Administration on implementation during 2003 of the plan 
for securing nuclear weapons, material, and expertise of the states of 
the former Soviet Union.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 February 8, 2005.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
February 9.
