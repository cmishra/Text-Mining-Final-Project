Statement on Pope John Paul II
February 24, 2005

    On behalf of all Americans, Laura and I send 
our heartfelt best wishes to Pope John Paul II. The Holy Father is in 
our thoughts and prayers, and we wish him a speedy recovery and return 
to the service of his church and all humanity.
