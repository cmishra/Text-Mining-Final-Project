Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on United States Trade and 
Investment Policy Toward Sub-Saharan Africa and Implementation of the 
African Growth and Opportunity Act
May 18, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 106 of title I of the Trade and Development 
Act of 2000 (Public Law 106-200), I transmit herewith the 2001 
Comprehensive Report of the President on U.S. Trade and Investment 
Policy toward Sub-Saharan Africa and Implementation of the African 
Growth and Opportunity Act.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 May 18, 2001.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
May 21.
