Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report on the North 
Atlantic Treaty
April 13, 1993

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    As requested in section 1314 of the National Defense Authorization 
Act for Fiscal Year 1993 (Public Law 102-484), I am forwarding the 
``Report on the North Atlantic Treaty of 1949.''
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
