Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on International 
Agreements
March 1, 2004

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. Chairman:)
    Consistent with subsection (b) of the Case-Zablocki Act, (1 U.S.C. 
112b), I hereby transmit a report prepared by the Department of State 
concerning international agreements.
         Sincerely,

                                                          George W. Bush

Note: Identical letters were sent to J. Dennis Hastert, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Richard G. Lugar, chairman, Senate 
Committee on Foreign Relations.
