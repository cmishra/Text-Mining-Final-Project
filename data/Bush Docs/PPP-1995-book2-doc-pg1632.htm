Message to the Congress Transmitting Budget Deferrals
October 19, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:
    In accordance with the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control 
Act of 1974, I herewith report three deferrals of budgetary resources, 
totaling $122.8 million.
    These deferrals affect the International Security Assistance 
program, and the Departments of Health and Human Services and State.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

October 19, 1995.
