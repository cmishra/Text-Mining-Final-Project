Message to the Congress Transmitting a Progress Report on Spending in 
Support of Plan Colombia
July 22, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:
    Consistent with section 3204(e), Public Law 106-246, I am providing 
a report prepared by my Administration detailing the progress of 
spending by the executive branch during the first two quarters of Fiscal 
Year 2003 in support of Plan Colombia.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 July 22, 2003.
