Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Vietnam
October 5, 1994

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    Pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 522 of the Foreign 
Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 1994 and 1995 (Public Law 103-
236), I hereby transmit the attached report on Sanctions on Vietnam.
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
