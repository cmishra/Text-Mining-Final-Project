Message to the Congress Transmitting the 1990 Report of the Commodity 
Credit Corporation
July 20, 1993

To the Congress of the United States:
    In accordance with the provisions of section 13, Public Law 806, 
80th Congress (15 U.S.C. 714k), I transmit herewith the report of the 
Commodity Credit Corporation for fiscal year 1990.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,
July 20, 1993.
