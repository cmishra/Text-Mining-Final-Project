Nomination for Ambassador to India
May 11, 1994

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Frank G. 
Wisner, of the District of Columbia, as Ambassador to India.
    ``I am pleased to announce Frank to this most important post,'' the 
President said. ``He brings an experienced background and skilled 
diplomacy to this important assignment.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
