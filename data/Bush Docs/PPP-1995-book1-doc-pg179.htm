Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the Operation of the 
Andean Trade Preference Act
February 8, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:
    I hereby submit the first report on the Operation of the Andean 
Trade Preference Act. This report is prepared pursuant to the 
requirements of section 203 of the Andean Trade Preference Act of 1991.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

February 8, 1995.
