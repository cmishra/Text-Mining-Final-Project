Statement on the Death of Police Officer Steve B. Favela
November 26, 2006

    Laura and I are deeply saddened by the death 
of Officer Steve Favela of the Honolulu Police Department. Officer 
Favela died from injuries he suffered while protecting us during our 
visit to Hawaii. We send our condolences to his wife, Barbara, his entire family, and his fellow law enforcement 
officers. We pray that God will comfort them and that their friends and 
loved ones will sustain them in this difficult time.
    Officer Favela risked his life every day to protect the people of 
his community. In this time of great sadness, we give thanks for his 
life of service.
