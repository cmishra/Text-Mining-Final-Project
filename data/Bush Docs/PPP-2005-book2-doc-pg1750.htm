Remarks Prior to Discussions With Premier Wen Jiabao of China in Beijing
November 20, 2005

    It's good to see you again, sir. I remember our visit very well. And 
I thank you for this invitation to come and talk and to have lunch. It 
will give us a chance

[[Page 1751]]

to continue to strengthen this very important relationship. And I agree 
with you; it's a relationship where we've got common interests. We don't 
agree on every issue, but we do agree we should discuss our differences 
and our likenesses in a cordial manner.
    And so I look forward to this meeting a lot. Thank you for having 
us.

Note: The President spoke at 1:07 p.m. at the Diaoyutai Guest House.
