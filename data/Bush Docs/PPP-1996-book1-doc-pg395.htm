Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on International 
Agreements
March 7, 1996

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. Chairman:)
    Pursuant to subsection (b) of the Case-Zablocki Act, (1 U.S.C. 
112b(b)), I hereby transmit a report prepared by the Department of State 
concerning international agreements.
        Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Newt Gingrich, Speaker of the House 
of Representatives, and Jesse Helms, Chairman of the Senate Committee on 
Foreign Relations. An original was not available. This letter was 
released by the Office of the Press Secretary on March 8.
