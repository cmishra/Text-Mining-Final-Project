Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the Operation of the 
Andean Trade Preference Act
February 5, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 203(f) of the Andean Trade Preference Act 
(APTA) of 1991, as amended (19 U.S.C. 3201 et seq.), I transmit herewith 
the third report to the Congress on the Operation of the Andean Trade 
Preference Act.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 February 5, 2001.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
February 6.
