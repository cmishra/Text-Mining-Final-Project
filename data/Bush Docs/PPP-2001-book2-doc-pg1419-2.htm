Message to the Congress Transmitting a Revision of the United States 
Arctic Research Plan
November 15, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    Pursuant to the provisions of the Arctic Research and Policy Act of 
1984, as amended (15 U.S.C. 4108(a)), I transmit herewith the seventh 
biennial revision (2002-2006) to the United States Arctic Research Plan.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 November 15, 2001.
