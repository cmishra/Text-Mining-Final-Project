Nomination for Ambassador to Zambia
May 25, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Roland Karl 
Kuchel to be Ambassador to Zambia. Kuchel, a career foreign service 
officer, is currently Assistant to the Director General of the Foreign 
Service.
    ``I am very glad to be making this nomination,'' said the President. 
``Roland Kuchel has had a long and accomplished career in the Foreign 
Service.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
