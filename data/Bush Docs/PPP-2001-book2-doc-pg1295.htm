Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report of the Railroad Retirement 
Board
October 23, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the Annual Report of the Railroad Retirement 
Board for Fiscal Year 2000, pursuant to the provisions of section 
7(b)(6) of the Railroad Retirement Act and section 12(1) of the Railroad 
Unemployment Insurance Act.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 October 23, 2001.
