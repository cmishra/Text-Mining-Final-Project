Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the National Emergency 
With Respect to Burma
May 16, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act, 50 
U.S.C. 1641(c), and section 204(c) of the International Emergency 
Economic Powers Act, 50 U.S.C. 1703(c), I am providing a report

[[Page 502]]

prepared by my Administration, covering the 6-month period since 
November 20, 2002, on the national emergency with respect to Burma that 
was declared in Executive Order 13047 of May 20, 1997.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 May 16, 2003.
