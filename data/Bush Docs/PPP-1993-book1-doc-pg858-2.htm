Nomination for Ambassador to Italy
June 15, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Reginald 
Bartholomew, a senior Foreign Service officer with the rank of Career 
Minister, to the post of Ambassador to Italy.
    ``Reg Bartholomew has served our country ably in several 
ambassadorial positions and many

[[Page 859]]

other challenging assignments,'' said the President. ``I have full 
confidence in his ability to maintain our strong relationship with 
Italy, an important ally whose friendship America highly values.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
