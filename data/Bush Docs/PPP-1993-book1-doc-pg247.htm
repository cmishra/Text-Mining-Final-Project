Nomination for Posts at the Departments of Health and Human Services and 
Education
March 5, 1993

    The President today announced his intention to nominate Bruce 
Vladeck to be Administrator of the Health Care Financing Administration 
at the Department of Health and Human Services and Norma Cantu to be 
Assistant Secretary for Civil Rights at the Department of Education.
    ``I am very pleased to make these nominations today to two extremely 
important positions,'' said the President. ``Bruce Vladeck and Norma 
Cantu are highly talented individuals with unique qualifications for the 
leadership roles that I have asked them to take.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
