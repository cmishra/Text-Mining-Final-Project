Message to the Congress Transmitting the Railroad Safety Report
April 20, 1993

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the 1991 annual report on the Administration of 
the Federal Railroad Safety Act of 1970, pursuant to section 211 of the 
Act (45 U.S.C. 440(a)).

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,
April 20, 1993.
