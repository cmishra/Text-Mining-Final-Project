Message to the Congress Transmitting the 2002 Trade Policy Agenda and 
2001 Report on the Trade Agreements Program
March 19, 2002

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 163 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended (19 
U.S.C. 2213), I transmit herewith the 2002 Trade Policy Agenda and 2001 
Annual Report on the Trade Agreements Program, as prepared by my 
Administration as of March 1, 2002.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 March 19, 2002.
