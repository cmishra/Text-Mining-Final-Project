Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on the National Emergency 
With Respect to Iran
March 12, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 401(c) of the National Emergencies Act, 50 
U.S.C. 1641(c), section 204(c) of the International Emergency Economic 
Powers Act, 50 U.S.C.

[[Page 262]]

1703(c), and section 505(c) of the International Security and 
Development Cooperation Act of 1985, 22 U.S.C. 2349aa-9(c), I am 
transmitting a 6-month periodic report prepared by my Administration on 
the national emergency with respect to Iran that was declared in 
Executive Order 12957 of March 15, 1995.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 March 12, 2003.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
March 13.
