Remarks Prior to Discussions With Prime Minister Andrus Ansip of Estonia in Tallinn, Estonia
November 28, 2006

[The Prime Minister's remarks are joined in progress.]

    Prime Minister Ansip. ----once again, thank you very much for your 
support and for the support of your country.

around the world. I've really been looking forward to coming here. I'm 
honored that you agreed to--[inaudible]. Thank you.
