Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the Railroad 
Retirement Board
September 27, 1996

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the Annual Report of the Railroad Retirement 
Board for Fiscal Year 1995, pursuant to the provisions of section 
7(b)(6) of the Railroad Retirement Act and section 12(1) of the Railroad 
Unemployment Insurance Act.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

September 27, 1996.
