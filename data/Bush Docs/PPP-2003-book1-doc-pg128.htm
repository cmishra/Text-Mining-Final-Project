Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Plan Colombia
February 4, 2003

To the Congress of the United States:
    Pursuant to Public Law 106-246, section 3204(e), I am providing a 
report prepared by my Administration detailing the progress of spending 
by the executive branch during the last two quarters of Fiscal Year 2002 
in support of Plan Colombia.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 February 4, 2003.
