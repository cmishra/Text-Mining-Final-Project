Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting the Report of the 
Trade and Development Agency
May 11, 1994

Dear Mr. Chairman:
    As required by section 201(d) of the Jobs Through Exports Act of 
1992 (Public Law 102-549; 22 U.S.C. 2421(d)), I transmit herewith the 
annual report of the Trade and Development Agency for fiscal year 1993.
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Claiborne Pell, chairman, Senate 
Committee on Foreign Relations, and Lee H. Hamilton, chairman, House 
Committee on Foreign Affairs.
