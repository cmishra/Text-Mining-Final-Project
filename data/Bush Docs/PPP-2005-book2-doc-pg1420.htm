Statement on the Electoral Victory of Prime Minister Junichiro 
Koizumi in Japan's General Elections
September 12, 2005

    I extend my congratulations to Prime Minister Koizumi for his 
decisive victory in yesterday's elections and to the Japanese people for 
continuing Japan's robust democratic tradition. Prime Minister Koizumi 
is a bold leader and a good friend, and I

[[Page 1421]]

look forward to continuing the close partnership between our two 
countries.
