Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the 
Corporation for Public Broadcasting
March 8, 1994

To the Congress of the United States:
    As required by section 19(3) of Public Telecommunications Act of 
1992 (Public Law 102-356), I transmit herewith the report of the 
Corporation for Public Broadcasting.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

March 8, 1994.
