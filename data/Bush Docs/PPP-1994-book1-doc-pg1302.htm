Nomination for United States District Court Judges
July 22, 1994

    The President today announced three nominees to serve on the U.S. 
District Court for the Eastern District of New York: Frederic Block, 
John Gleeson, and Allyne R. Ross.
    ``These three individuals have demonstrated a profound commitment to 
the law,'' the President said. ``I know they will be of great service to 
the State of New York and to this Nation.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
