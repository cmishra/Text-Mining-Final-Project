Statement on the Resignation of William H. Donaldson as Chairman of the Securities and Exchange Commission
June 1, 2005

    Bill Donaldson took on a tough job at a tough time, and he delivered 
for the American people. He vigorously and fairly enforced our Nation's 
securities laws and helped rebuild the public trust in corporate America 
that has been important to our economic recovery. I am grateful for his 
dedicated service, and Laura and I wish him and 
Jane all the best.
