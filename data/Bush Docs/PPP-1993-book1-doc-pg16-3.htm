Nomination for Director of the Office of Personnel Management
January 26, 1993

    The President today announced that he intends to nominate Jim King 
as Director of the Office of Personnel Management.
    ``Jim King will bring invaluable expertise, knowledge, and 
enthusiasm to the Office of Personnel Management,'' said President 
Clinton.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
