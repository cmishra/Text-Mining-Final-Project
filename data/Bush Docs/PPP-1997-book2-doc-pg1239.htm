Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on Future
Free Trade Area Negotiations
September 25, 1997

Dear Mr. Chairman:
    In accordance with section 108(b)(4) of the North American Free 
Trade Agreement Implementation Act (19 U.S.C. 3317(b)(4)), I transmit 
herewith the report containing recommendations on future free trade area 
negotiations.
        Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Bill Archer, chairman, House 
Committee on Ways and Means, and William V. Roth, Jr., chairman, Senate 
Finance Committee.
