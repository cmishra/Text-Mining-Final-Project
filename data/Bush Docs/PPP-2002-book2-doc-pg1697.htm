Statement on the Death of Representative Patsy T. Mink
September 30, 2002

    Laura and I are saddened by the news of the passing of Congresswoman 
Patsy Mink. Our thoughts and prayers are with her family and friends. 
Patsy Mink fought tirelessly for the causes she supported, and our 
Nation is grateful to her for her long record of public service.
