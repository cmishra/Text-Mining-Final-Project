Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Plan Colombia
July 26, 2001

To the Congress of the United States:
    Pursuant to section 3204(e) of Public Law 106-246, I hereby transmit 
a report detailing the progress of spending by the executive branch 
during the first two quarters of Fiscal Year 2001 in support of Plan 
Colombia.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 July 26, 2001.
