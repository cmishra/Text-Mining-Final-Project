Nomination for United States District Court Judges
August 5, 1994

    The President today nominated two individuals to serve on the U.S. 
District Court: Robert N. Chatigny for the District of Connecticut and 
Judith D. McConnell for the Southern District of California.
    ``Each of these nominees has an exceptional record of legal 
achievement,'' the President said. ``I know they will serve on the 
Federal bench with distinction.''

Note: Biographies of the nominees were made available by the Office of 
the Press Secretary.
