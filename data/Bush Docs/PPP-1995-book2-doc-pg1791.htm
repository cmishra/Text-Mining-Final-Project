Message to the Congress Transmitting the Railroad Retirement Board 
Report
November 28, 1995

To the Congress of the United States:
    I transmit herewith the Annual Report of the Railroad Retirement 
Board for Fiscal Year 1994, pursuant to the provisions of section 
7(b)(6) of the Railroad Retirement Act and section 12(1) of the Railroad 
Unemployment Insurance Act.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

November 28, 1995.
