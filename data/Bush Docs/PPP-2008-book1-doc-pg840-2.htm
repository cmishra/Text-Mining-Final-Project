Message to the Congress on the Designation of the Chairman and Vice 
Chairman of the United States International Trade Commission
June 17, 2008

To the Congress of the United States:
    Consistent with the provisions of 19 U.S.C. 1330(c)(1), this is to 
notify the Congress that I have designated Shara L. Aranoff as Chairman and Daniel Pearson as Vice Chairman of the United States International 
Trade Commission, effective June 17, 2008.

                                                          George W. Bush

 The White House,

 June 17, 2008.
