Statement on the Death of James Brown
December 25, 2006

    Laura and I are saddened by the death of 
James Brown. For half a century, the innovative talent of the 
``Godfather of Soul''

[[Page 2224]]

enriched our culture and influenced generations of musicians. An 
American original, his fans came from all walks of life and backgrounds. 
James Brown's family and friends are in our thoughts and prayers this 
Christmas.
