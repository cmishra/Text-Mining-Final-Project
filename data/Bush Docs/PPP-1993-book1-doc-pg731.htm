Exchange With Reporters on the Economic Program
May 25, 1993

    Q. Mr. President, is the House going to pass your tax bill?
    The President. I think they're going to pass the budget bill, yes, 
which has a lot of cuts in it, and it also has some good things for 
these folks, good for manufacturing, good for small business. Good bill.

Note: The exchange began at 2 p.m. at the National Air and Space Museum. 
A tape was not available for verification of the content of this 
exchange.
