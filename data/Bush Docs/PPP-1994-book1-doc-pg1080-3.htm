Nomination for the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission
June 14, 1994

    The President today announced his intent to nominate Gilbert F. 
Casellas as Chairman of the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission.
    ``Gilbert Casellas' dedication and commitment to providing equal 
opportunities to all Americans and his skilled ability to build 
consensus around even the most complex issues make me proud to nominate 
him to chair this essential commission,'' the President said.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
