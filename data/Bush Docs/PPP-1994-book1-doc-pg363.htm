Message to the Congress Transmitting the Report of the 
Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee
March 2, 1994

To the Congress of the United States:

    Pursuant to the provisions of section 108(b) of Public Law 98-373 
(15 U.S.C. 4107(b)), I transmit herewith the Fifth Biennial Report of 
the Interagency Arctic Research Policy Committee (February 1, 1992, to 
January 31, 1994).

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

March 2, 1994.
