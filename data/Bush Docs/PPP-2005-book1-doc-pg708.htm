Memorandum on Redesignation of the United States Naval Reserve to the 
United States Navy Reserve
April 29, 2005

Memorandum for the Secretary of Defense

Subject: Redesignation of the United States Naval Reserve to the United 
States Navy Reserve

    Pursuant to the Ronald W. Reagan National Defense Authorization Act 
for Fiscal Year 2005 (Public Law 108-375), I approve the redesignation 
of the United States Naval Reserve to the United States Navy Reserve.

                                                          George W. Bush
