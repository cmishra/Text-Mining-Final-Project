Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on International 
Agreements
March 15, 1994

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. Chairman:)
    Pursuant to subsection (b) of the Case- Zablocki Act (1 U.S.C. 
section 112b(b)), I transmit herewith a report prepared by the 
Department of State concerning international agreements.
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Claiborne Pell, Chairman of the Senate 
Foreign Relations Committee.
