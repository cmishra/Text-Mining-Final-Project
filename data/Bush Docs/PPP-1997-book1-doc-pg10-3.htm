Message to the Congress Transmitting a Report on Hazardous Materials 
Transportation
January 7, 1997

To the Congress of the United States:
    In accordance with Public Law 103-272, as amended (49 U.S.C. 
5121(e)), I transmit herewith the Biennial Report on Hazardous Materials 
Transportation for Calendar Years 1994-1995 of the Department of 
Transportation.

                                                      William J. Clinton

The White House,

January 7, 1997.

Note: This message was released by the Office of the Press Secretary on 
January 8.
