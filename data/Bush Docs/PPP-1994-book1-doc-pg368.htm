Statement on Disaster Assistance for Alabama
March 3, 1994

    My heart goes out to the people of the South who have suffered from 
these recent storms. I know how difficult this winter has been, and I am 
confident that FEMA Director James Lee Witt will do all he can to assist 
the people of Alabama, and those throughout the rest of the South, to 
recover from these damaging storms.

Note: This statement was included in a White House statement announcing 
disaster assistance for Alabama due to winter storms that began January 
14 and continued through February 14.
