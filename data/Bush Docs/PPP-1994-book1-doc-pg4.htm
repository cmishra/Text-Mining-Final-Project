Letter to Congressional Leaders Transmitting a Report on 
Loan Guarantees to Israel
January 3, 1994

Dear Mr. Speaker:  (Dear Mr. President:)
    Enclosed is an unclassified report on the Loan Guarantees to Israel 
Program as required by section 226(k) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 
1961, as amended (Public Law 87-195).
    I hope this report will be useful to you.
    Sincerely,

                                                      William J. Clinton

Note: Identical letters were sent to Thomas S. Foley, Speaker of the 
House of Representatives, and Albert Gore, Jr., President of the Senate.
