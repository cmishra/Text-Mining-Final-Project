Nomination for the National Labor Relations Board
February 3, 1994

    The President announced his intention today to nominate Charles I. 
Cohen to be a member of the National Labor Relations Board.
    ``Charles Cohen is a respected attorney with years of experience on 
the NLRB staff. I believe he will be an effective member of the Board,'' 
said the President.

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
