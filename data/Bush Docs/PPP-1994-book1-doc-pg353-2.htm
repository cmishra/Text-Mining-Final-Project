Nomination for the Small Business Administration
March 1, 1994

    The President today nominated Helen Dixon as the Regional Director 
for Region V at the Small Business Administration.
    ``I am pleased to nominate Helen to the position of Regional 
Director,'' the President said, ``Her firsthand experience with small 
business

[[Page 354]]

will be a great asset to our SBA programs in the Midwest.''

Note: A biography of the nominee was made available by the Office of the 
Press Secretary.
